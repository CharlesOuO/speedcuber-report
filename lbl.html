Layer By Layer (LBL)

The foundational method for solving the 3x3x3 Cube.

Overview

The **Layer By Layer (LBL)** method is the most popular method for beginners. Click on the icons below to jump to the specific step.

Detailed Steps

01

Cross

Hold White Center Down. Locate the four white edge pieces. Align the side color with the center, then rotate it down to match the white center.

02

First Layer Corners

Find a white corner in the top layer and align it above its target slot.

  • White Facing Up: Use "Sexy Move": R U R' U'
  • Facing Front (Right): U R U' R'
  • Facing Front (Left): U' L' U L
03

Second Layer

Find an edge in the top layer without yellow. Align it with the center color to form a "T".

  • Target is Right: U R U' R' ... U' L' U L
  • Target is Left: U' L' U L ... U R U' R'
04

2-Step OLL

1. Make Yellow Cross: F (R U R' U') F'

2. Orient Corners (Sune): R U R' U R U2 R'

05

2-Step PLL

1. Permute Corners (T-Perm): R U R' U' R' F R2 U' R' U' R U R' F'

2. Permute Edges (U-Perm): R U' R U R U R U' R' U' R2
