05

2-Step PLL

Step 1: Permute Corners (Ja Perm)

  • Headlights found: Put them on the LEFT, then do the algorithm.
  • No Headlights: Do the algorithm from any angle. Headlights will appear.

Ja Perm

R' U L' U2 R U' R' U2 R L

Step 2: Permute Edges (U Perm)

Note: If the cube is not solved after the first time, simply do the algorithm again.

U Perm

R' U' R U R' U2 R2 U R' U R U2 R'
