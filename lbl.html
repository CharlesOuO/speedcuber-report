Layer By Layer (LBL)

The foundational method for solving the 3x3x3 Cube.

Overview

The **Layer By Layer (LBL)** method is the most popular method for beginners. Click on the icons below to jump to the specific step.

Detailed Steps

01

Cross

Hold White Center Down. Locate the four white edge pieces. Align the side color with the center, then rotate it down to match the white center.

02

First Layer Corners

Find a white corner in the top layer and align it above its target slot.

  • White Facing Up:
    Use "Sexy Move": R U R' U'
  • White Facing Front (Right):
    Algorithm: U R U' R'
  • White Facing Front (Left):
    Algorithm: U' L' U L
Image: Completed First Layer
03

Second Layer

Find an edge in the top layer without yellow. Align it with the center color to form a "T".

  • Target is Right:
    U R U' R' ... (rotate cube) ... U' L' U L
  • Target is Left:
    U' L' U L ... (rotate cube) ... U R U' R'
Image: Completed First Two Layers
04

2-Step OLL (Yellow Face)

1. Make Yellow Cross:
If "L-shape" or "Line": F (R U R' U') F'

2. Orient Corners (Sune):
Repeat until full yellow face:
R U R' U R U2 R'

Image: Yellow Face Complete
05

2-Step PLL (Finish)

1. Permute Corners (T-Perm):
Put matching corners (headlights) on left:
R U R' U' R' F R2 U' R' U' R U R' F'

2. Permute Edges (U-Perm):
Cycle edges counter-clockwise:
R U' R U R U R U' R' U' R2
