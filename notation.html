Cube Notation

Learn how to read the algorithms (R, U, L, F...).

How to Read

Speedcubing algorithms are written using letters. Each letter represents a face of the cube and how you should turn it.

Rule of Thumb: Imagine you are facing that specific side. "Clockwise" means turning it like a clock's hands.

Basic Moves (Clockwise)

R (Right)

Turn the Right face clockwise (Upwards).

L (Left)

Turn the Left face clockwise (Downwards).

U (Up)

Turn the Top face clockwise (To the Left).

D (Down)

Turn the Bottom face clockwise (To the Right).

F (Front)

Turn the Front face clockwise.

B (Back)

Turn the Back face clockwise.

Modifiers (' and 2)

Prime ( ' )

An apostrophe means Counter-Clockwise.

Example: R'

Turn the Right face downwards.

Double ( 2 )

The number 2 means turn the face Twice (180 degrees).

Example: U2

Turn the Top face twice.
