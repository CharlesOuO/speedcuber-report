Cube Notation

Complete guide to reading speedcubing algorithms.

Algorithms are sequences of moves. Each letter represents a face or layer.
Apostrophe (') means Counter-Clockwise.
Number 2 means turn 180 degrees (Double turn).

1
Basic
2
Wide
3
Slice
4
Rotate
01

Basic Face Turns

Turn the outer face 90 degrees. (Apostrophe ' = Counter-Clockwise)

02

Wide Moves (2 Layers)

Turn two layers at once. Denoted by lowercase letter or 'w'.

03

Slice Moves (M, E, S)

Turn the middle layers only.

04

Cube Rotations (x, y, z)

Rotate the entire puzzle to face a new side (Regrip).
