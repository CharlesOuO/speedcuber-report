3x3 One-Handed

My Main Event: Specialized Finger Tricks & RU-Gen Algs.

One-Handed (OH) solving is my specialty. I focus on smooth turning and high TPS. Unlike many solvers, I do not use the table for stability; I prefer a fully floating style with precise grip control.

1. My Finger Tricks

Ring Finger (R / R')

I primarily use my Ring Finger for R' moves (pulling down) and stabilizing the cube.

Index Push (U / U')

For U moves, I use my index finger to push or pull. E.g., Pushing FL to BL for U moves.

Floating Style

I solve without resting the cube on the table. This requires stronger grip strength.

Stable Grip

Keeping the thumb on the front center is crucial for seamless transitions.

2. Full 57 OLL Algorithms

Standard RU-Gen focused algorithms for OH.

Advanced Strategy (COLL): Using COLL (Corners of Last Layer) algorithms can significantly improve OH performance. By solving OLL and permuting corners simultaneously, you are guaranteed to get an EPLL (Edge Permutation only) like U, H, or Z perms, which are much faster and easier to execute one-handed.
