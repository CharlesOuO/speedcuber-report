CFOP Method

Advanced speedcubing technique for faster solves.

Overview

The **CFOP (Fridrich)** Method is widely used by speedcubers globally. It divides the solve into four major stages: Cross, F2L, OLL, and PLL. It is significantly faster than LBL because it combines steps.

Strategy: Focus on look-ahead during F2L to reduce pause times.

The 4 Stages

C

Cross

Build the cross directly on the bottom (D-face) to save rotation time. Advanced solvers plan the entire cross during inspection.

F

F2L (First 2 Layers)

Instead of solving corners and edges separately, pair them up and insert them into slots simultaneously. This covers 41 basic cases.

O

OLL (Orientation)

Orient all pieces on the top layer so the top face is a single color (usually yellow). There are 57 algorithms for 1-look OLL.
